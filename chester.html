Chester

Rustic wooden oak chess table, designed for both functionality and aesthetic appeal. This unique piece features bark-trimmed edges, giving it a natural, rustic charm. The chessboard is meticulously burned into the table's surface, ensuring durability and elegance. The table stands on sleek hairpin legs, blending modern design with rustic elements seamlessly. The chess pieces are handcrafted from cement, with a minimalist cubic design that stands out through subtle, distinctive details. For instance, the queen features a small light bulb, adding a charming and innovative touch.

  • Queen: mini lightbulb
  • King: gold flanged hex head bolt
  • Pawn: small cube
  • Rook: big cube
  • Knight: tbd
  • Bishop: tbd
