Barney

Barney is an office desk transformed from an old barn door I stumbled upon in a cellar. Covered in cobwebs this door had so much potential waiting to be uncovered. After careful cleaning and sawing to fit just right, I spent some time sanding away imperfections to reveal its natural beauty. To preserve its rustic charm, I applied a transparent, glossy spar varnish, adding a subtle shine while protecting its unique character. Complementing the door's rustic charm are its simple black legs, which are height-adjustable to transition from sitting to standing while working, promoting a healthier and more dynamic workspace experience.
