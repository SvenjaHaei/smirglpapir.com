Barney

Barney was an old barn door that I found in a cellar covered with spider webs. After cleaning, sawing it to the right size (120x80cm) and multiple rounds of sanding I applied a tranparent, shiny spar varnish (Bauhaus). The legs are simple, black and height-adjustable (Flexispot).
